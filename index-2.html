Greece

Athens & the isles

We started where the stories start. Marble columns and the old Olympic track, an afternoon that turned into a sail, and a pink little corner of Athens that felt like walking into a storybook. The kids stood on the winners' podium. We let them.

The kind of blue you don't believe until you're standing in it.

STOP 02

Jamaica

18.47° N, 77.92° W
Martha Brae River

From ancient stone to running water. A bamboo raft, a slow current under a stone bridge, and absolutely nowhere to be. This was the exhale of the whole summer.

Poled down the river by hand, one bamboo push at a time.
No motor, no rush. Just green water, the guide's steady rhythm, and sun.
